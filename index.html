About the Event

A dynamic competition to create awareness about the Necessity of Hydrogen, biofuels, and other renewable energy sources-based start-ups in the Indian Scenario.

Exclusively for school students studying in XI or XII (State, CBSE and ICSE).

Competitions

Prize: ₹3500

Quiz

Topic: Renewable energy sources (hydrogen, solar, wind, geothermal, biofuels) and Renewable Energy Sources Based Start-ups.

Prize: ₹3500

Start-up Idea Presentation

Topic: Creation of new energy technologies based on renewable sources (e.g., renewable fuel-powered vehicles, replacing LPG, hydrogen/biofuel production, waste-to-energy).

Prize: ₹3500

Debate

Topic: Necessity of Hydrogen, Biofuels and other Renewable Energy Sources Based Start-ups in Indian Scenario.

Please Note

  • The participants must be exclusively from XI or XII (State, CBSE and ICSE).
  • Only one team (of two students) is allowed from a school for each competition — a school cannot send two teams for the same competition.
  • Schools may register the same team for all three competitions or send a separate team for each competition.
  • Debaters must speak exclusively on the topic. Countering infeasible ideas with justification is allowed.
  • Winners adjudicated by juries will be named HEAM Parliamentarian 2026.
  • All participants will receive a participation certificate.
